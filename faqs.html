In practice, there are several ways to answer the question of how much my second-hand car is worth? . Because one thing is the market value and another is the market value of a car .

GANVAM is the National Association of Sellers, Repair and Spare Parts. GANVAM car valuation tables offer valuation for all car models and versions. Therefore, to know how much my car is worth according to GANVAM, you have to visit these tables. At Canalcar we carry out vehicle appraisals based on these tables so that we can calculate the market value of a car .

Hacienda has some car price tables. So if you are wondering how much my car is worth according to the Treasury , you should know a few things. Because the market value of a car is calculated based on the tables with amounts determined by this Ministry. These values ​​are published regularly updated in the Official State Gazette (BOE). To establish the market value, it takes into account the age, make, model and version of the used car.
